About Us

Every day, over 300 Americans are victims of gun violence. At Warden, our goal is to help reduce the number of gun violence victims through the detection of pistols using our artificial intelligence powered model. This model has been trained with over 2500 images to detect when a pistol is in a certain room. This technology can serve as an at-home security feature or even in the biggest airports in the world in order to reduce potential gun threats. With Warden, safety speaks louder than silence.
